Methods 2022 - Panel Questions

  • What is the most common mistake you have seen from newcomers to the industry like us?
  • If you had to pick the one most beneficial thing you did in your past that has led you to where you are now, what would it be?
  • Have you had to deal with imposter syndrome? If so how often and does it ever go away? Or does it keep morphing into new things?
  • What is something that you wish Computer Science students were taught in school?
  • What is your opinion on people moving from job to job throughout their career? It seems to be the new wave to ride, but how does it look to people with long-ish careers
  • Is it really an employees market right now? Should I be picky when looking for a job, or should I just be glad to get a few offers?
